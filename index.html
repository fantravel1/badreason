Informal Fallacy

Straw Man

Misrepresenting someone's argument to make it easier to attack by exaggerating, misrepresenting, or just completely fabricating it.

Example:

Person A: We should give more funding to schools. Person B: So you think we should waste money on frivolous programs instead of fixing roads? That's irresponsible!
